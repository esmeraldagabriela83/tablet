Antibiotics

An antibiotic is a type of antimicrobial substance active against bacteria.
It is the most important type of antibacterial agent for fighting bacterial infections, and antibiotic medications are widely used in the treatment and prevention of such infections.
They may either kill or inhibit the growth of bacteria. A limited number of antibiotics also possess antiprotozoal activity.

Drugs

There are many hundreds of thousands of possible drugs.
Any chemical substance with biological activity may be considered a drug. This list categorises drugs alphabetically and also by other categorisations.

Insulin

Insulina este un hormon, secretat de celulele beta ale pancreasului, ce participă la metabolismul glucidelor.
Insulina este cel mai important hormon în metabolismul glucidelor. Insulina contribuie în primul rând la micșorarea concentrației glucozei în sânge.
Aceasta mărește permeabilitatea membranei celulare pentru glucoză. Insulina are o acțiunea antagonistă glucagonului.
